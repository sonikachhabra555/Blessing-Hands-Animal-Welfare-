About Us

Blessing Hands Animal Welfare Society is dedicated to the rescue, treatment, and care of injured and abandoned animals. With love, we provide medical help, food, shelter, and adoption services.

Our Work

  • Rescue injured animals 24/7
  • Provide sterilization and vaccination
  • Lost & Found tracking and reunions
  • Emergency ambulance and vet care
  • Food ATM for street dogs using plastic bottle exchange
